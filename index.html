NeoWallJump is a game inspired by Super Metroid+Matrix made during the GlobalJam 2018 (48 Hour Hack- athon) in Toronto. It is simple game made using JavaScript/Pixi/p2.js . The objective of the game is to get to the top of the wall through wall-jumps while avoiding the falling objects. To perform a wall-jump, you have to time the jump key (up button) correctly with the proper sprite animation frame.
